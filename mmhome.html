Save Our Oceans

Overview

College students are one of the most influential groups in shaping environmental change. Campuses are powerful communities where ideas spread quickly and change happens fast.Ocean pollution is an evergrowing issue, and one might wonder how it personally affects them. Pollution of the ocean poses threats to human health, the flow of ecosystems, and even the economy. By reducing waste, choosing reusable products, and raising awareness, you can help shift entire groups toward protecting the ocean. While it may not always be easy to see, protecting the oceans also protects people.
